About MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, known as Thala to his fans, is one of the greatest captains in cricket history. A leader, a finisher, and a legend who brought glory to India and Chennai Super Kings (CSK). His calm demeanor, lightning-fast stumpings, and incredible leadership led India to many victories and CSK to 5 IPL trophies.

Achievements

  • Won ICC T20 World Cup (2007)
  • Won ICC Cricket World Cup (2011)
  • Won ICC Champions Trophy (2013)
  • Led CSK to 5 IPL titles (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023)
  • Only captain to win all ICC white-ball trophies

Matches Played

  • 90 Tests
  • 350 ODIs
  • 98 T20Is
  • 250+ IPL matches

The helicopter shot, made famous by Dhoni, involves a powerful flick of the wrists and a whip-like swing that sends the ball flying. It's iconic and unmatched in style.
